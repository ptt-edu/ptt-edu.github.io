Welcome to Philadelphia Technician Training Institute

During these uncertain economic times, it is important for education programs to focus on helping their graduates find jobs and careers. That's why students in Philadelphia are looking for the best trade school.

PTTI was founded 15 years ago with the belief that young people should receive an education focused on technology. This education can help them secure good technical careers and find direction in their lives. PTTI has grown thanks to its dedicated employees and faculty who graduated from various universities and colleges.

At PTTI, the programs emphasize practical training with a combination of online and classroom learning. In the 21st century, it is essential to know how to work with trade and technology in our daily lives in order to find stable employment. PTTI supports its students in acquiring these skills.

There are many program options available at PTTI. We offer seven different independent programs in various industries, including:

  • Welding
  • Pipe Fitting
  • Manufacturing and Automation
  • Concreting and Masonry
  • Sterile Processing
  • Automotive
  • Drywall Framing

PTTI sets high expectations for success through appropriate technical education that considers individual differences and learning styles. This helps students develop technology skills and secure long-term employment in diverse industries.

If you're looking to receive a technology-driven education that can lead to a successful career, PTTI is the place for you. Our hands-on training, combined with online and classroom learning, prepares students for the demands of the 21st-century job market. Whether you're interested in automotive repair, manufacturing, welding, or other industries, PTTI offers a range of programs to suit your goals. Take the first step towards securing your future by joining PTTI today. Don't miss out on the opportunity to acquire valuable technology skills and pave the way for long-term employment.
